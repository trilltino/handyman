# XF TRADESMEN - VEO AI VIDEO TRAILER
## Marketing Video Production Brief

---

## **PROJECT OVERVIEW**

**Video Title:** "From Tools to Traffic: The XF Tradesmen Story"  
**Duration:** 60-90 seconds  
**Platform:** Veo AI Video Generation  
**Target Audience:** UK Tradespeople (electricians, plumbers, carpenters, handymen, gas engineers, roofers, etc.)  
**Objective:** Drive website purchases by showcasing the transformation from struggling for visibility to thriving with a professional online presence

---

## **NARRATIVE ARC: 4-SCENE STRUCTURE**

### **SCENE 1: THE GRIND (0-15 seconds)**
**Visual Setting:** Real job site - cluttered van, toolbox, handyman finishing a repair job  
**Time:** Late afternoon, natural lighting  
**Mood:** Honest, hardworking, but tired

**ACTION:**
- Handyman (40s, wearing work clothes, tool belt) tightening final screws on a kitchen cabinet installation
- Close-up of hands receiving cash payment from satisfied homeowner
- Handyman nods, wipes sweat, packs tools into worn van

**VOICEOVER (Handyman's internal thoughts):**
> "Another day, another £150. Good money... but where's the next job coming from?"

**ON-SCREEN TEXT:**
- "Dave - Local Handyman, Coventry"
- "£40K/year... but wants more"

**EMOTION:** Satisfaction mixed with uncertainty

---

### **SCENE 2: THE PROBLEM (15-30 seconds)**
**Visual Setting:** Home office/kitchen table - evening  
**Time:** Night, warm lamp lighting  
**Mood:** Frustrated but determined

**ACTION:**
- Dave sits at kitchen table with laptop, scrolling through competitor websites
- Opens browser tabs: "get more customers handyman", "advertise plumbing business"  
- Frustrated expression looking at expensive marketing options (£500/month ads showing)
- Wife walks past, pats shoulder supportively

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Facebook ads cost a fortune... word of mouth isn't enough anymore. Every plumber in Coventry has a flashy website except me."

**ON-SCREEN TEXT (appearing as he researches):**
- "£500/month for Google Ads?" ❌
- "Website builders too complicated" ❌  
- "Can't afford an agency" ❌

**KEY MOMENT:** Dave types "affordable tradesman website UK" into search

**EMOTION:** Frustration turning to hope

---

### **SCENE 3: THE DISCOVERY (30-50 seconds)**
**Visual Setting:** Same kitchen table - screen reflection in eyes  
**Time:** Still night  
**Mood:** Excitement building

**ACTION:**
- Screen shows XFTradesmen.com landing page loading
- Camera zooms into Dave's face - eyes widen
- Mouse clicks through pricing page
- Calculator open showing £329 + £30/month math
- Dave's expression shifts from skeptical to impressed

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Wait... £329 setup, then just £30 a month? That's less than one small job. And it's already built for tradesmen?"

**ON-SCREEN DYNAMIC TEXT (appearing as features):**
✅ **MOBILE-RESPONSIVE DESIGN**  
  *(Phone screen mockup appears)*

✅ **LOCAL SEO OPTIMIZATION**  
  *(Google Maps pin animation)*

✅ **AUTO-BOOKING SYSTEM**  
  *(Calendar filling with bookings)*

✅ **24/7 LEAD CAPTURE**  
  *(Notification bells ringing)*

✅ **GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE SETUP**  
  *(5-star reviews appearing)*

✅ **LIVE IN 48 HOURS**  
  *(Countdown timer: 2 days)*

**KEY SELLING POINTS (rapid-fire visuals):**
- "Custom domain: davehandyman.co.uk" 
- "No contracts - cancel anytime"  
- "Built on Rust architecture" (tech credibility)
- "Serving: Electricians | Plumbers | Carpenters | All Trades"

**MUSIC CUE:** Upbeat, hopeful electronic build

**EMOTION:** "This is it" realization

---

### **SCENE 4: THE TRANSFORMATION (50-90 seconds)**
**Visual Setting:** Split screen / montage  
**Time:** 6 months later  
**Mood:** Success, confidence, growth

**ACTION SEQUENCE:**

**Visual A - Website Live:**
- Laptop screen showing Dave's live website: **davehandyman.co.uk**
- Professional homepage with before/after gallery
- Contact form with "3 new enquiries" notification
- Mobile view showing instant quote requests

**Visual B - Business Growth:**
- Dave's phone buzzing with booking notifications while working
- New branded van (still modest but cleaner, logo on side)
- Dave shaking hands with new customer at doorstep
- Calendar app full of color-coded jobs

**Visual C - Financial Impact:**
- Subtle graph showing revenue climbing
- Dave at same kitchen table, now smiling, showing wife the phone
- Screen shows "15 jobs booked this month via website"

**VOICEOVER (Dave, confident):**
> "Best £329 I ever spent. The website pays for itself with ONE job. But I'm getting 15+ enquiries a month now. People find me on Google, book online, and I just show up and do what I do best."

**RAPID-FIRE TESTIMONIAL OVERLAYS:**
- "Found on Google first page for 'plumber Coventry'" ✓
- "Booked 3 jobs while I was sleeping" ✓  
- "Customers say I look professional now" ✓

**FINAL MOMENT:**
- Dave clicks "Mark as Complete" on job booking
- Looks directly at camera with slight smile
- Nods (the same nod from Scene 1, but now it means something different)

**EMOTION:** Quiet triumph

## **KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES**

### **Emotional Hooks:**
1. **Relatability:** Dave is every tradesperson - skilled but invisible online
2. **Affordability:** £329 is less scary than £5,000 agency quotes
3. **Speed:** 48 hours = immediate gratification
4. **Freedom:** No contracts = low risk
5. **Results:** Real bookings = tangible ROI

### **Features to Emphasize:**
- **Price Transparency:** £329 + £30/month (repeat 3x in video)
- **Speed to Launch:** "48 hours" (mentioned twice)
- **Local SEO:** "Get found on Google in your area"
- **Mobile-First:** "80% of customers search on phones"
- **All Trades:** Electricians, plumbers, carpenters, gas engineers, roofers, painters, locksmiths, HVAC
- **No Tech Skills Needed:** "We build it, you just send your details"
- **Support Included:** "24/7 support, updates, hosting - all in £30/month"

### **Psychological Triggers:**
1. **Pain Point:** Not getting enough jobs
2. **Competitor Pressure:** "Every other plumber has a website"
3. **Lost Revenue:** "How many customers found someone else because you weren't online?"
4. **Social Proof:** "100+ tradespeople" (implied testimonials)
5. **Risk Reversal:** "Cancel anytime. No lock-in"
